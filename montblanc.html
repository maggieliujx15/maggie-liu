MONTBLANC

Montblanc, a legacy brand renowned for its mastery in luxury writing instruments, has continuously evolved to cater to the modern achiever, creative, and challenger. The brand embarked on a bold journey to merge the timeless elegance of handwriting with cutting-edge digital experiences of an eINK device.

We collaborated closely with Montblanc's team, guiding the UX/UI journey from wireframe review to final design handoff.

Immersion

To kick off the project, we spent a week embedded with the Montblanc team in Hamburg — an immersive experience where we explored the brand's rich history, delved into their archives, and interacted with early prototypes of the device. This immersion helped us deeply understand Montblanc's values, craft, and ambition for their new writing device.

The Beauty of Handwriting

We began by exploring four distinct visual territories to ensure we were heading in the right direction. This process helped us narrow down to two creative concepts, ultimately leading to the final visual identity.

Capturing the beauty and act of handwriting in a digital space, allowing you to leave your mark for the long haul. This concept blends the uniqueness of the writing instruments with the Montblanc's heritage.

Values: Soulfulness, timelessness and simplicity.

Type

Ligatures bring a handcrafted realism to digital handwriting by mimicking the natural flow and variation of letters. By designing on paper, we capture the true essence of ink and imperfection — delivering a uniquely authentic, handwrite-like experience in digital form.

The Line

The line embraces the craft and DNA of writing, with all its perfections and imperfections.

For over a hundred years, the figure 8 has been used by trained calligraphers to test the nibs at the start of every pen's journey. We've brought this iconic gesture to the eInk experience as a way to introduce users to the feel of the device.

Buttons & Links

UI elements that are used in repetition (e.g. highlight states for pagination) will be created with stroke variations to represent the pressure of ink flowing on the UI.

Strokes can vary in starting/end points and width.

Iconography

Our icons reflect the essence of the line — capturing subtle imperfections from pen pressure and angle. While they nod to the tactile nature of handwriting, they remain clean and modern, balancing handcrafted warmth with UI clarity.

Colour

Our colour system is optimized for eInk, using a refined grayscale palette to ensure clarity, contrast, and a quiet, premium feel.

Layouts & Grids

Inspired by Montblanc's peak at 4810 metres, our layout aligns this symbolic number with a responsive column structure: 4 columns for mobile, 8 for tablet, and 10 for desktop.

Illustrations

These are visual direction and references to guide the illustration style. We aim to strike a balance between function and emotion. Handwritten lines reflect the practical side of note-taking, while hand-sketched elements bring emotional depth and crafted sophistication.

Interaction Model

We defined the interaction model based on market research of current eInk devices, ensuring an intuitive and seamless user experience.

Onboarding Flow

To ease users into the eINK experience, the onboarding flow blends Montblanc's heritage storytelling with moments of playfulness. This approach not only introduces key device features, but also reflects the brand's craftsmanship and attention to detail. We thoroughly tested the flow—evaluating its structure, length, and clarity—to ensure it was both engaging and informative. The goal was to help users intuitively understand how eINK technology works while creating a welcoming, on-brand first interaction with the device.
