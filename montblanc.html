MONTBLANC

Montblanc, a legacy brand renowned for its mastery in luxury writing instruments, has continuously evolved to cater to the modern achiever, creative, and challenger. The brand embarked on a bold journey to merge the timeless elegance of handwriting with cutting-edge digital experiences of an eINK device.

We collaborated closely with Montblanc's team, guiding the UX/UI journey from wireframe review to final design handoff.

Immersion

To kick off the project, we spent a week embedded with the Montblanc team in Hamburg — an immersive experience where we explored the brand's rich history, delved into their archives, and interacted with early prototypes of the device. This immersion helped us deeply understand Montblanc's values, craft, and ambition for their new writing device.

The Beauty of Handwriting

We set out to capture the soulfulness of handwriting in a digital context. Inspired by Montblanc's long heritage in fine writing instruments, we explored four distinct visual territories to define the right direction. This exploration helped us narrow down to two core creative concepts, ultimately shaping the final visual identity.

Our goal was to translate the emotional resonance of ink on paper into an eInk environment—preserving the natural imperfections, pressure, and personal expression that make handwriting uniquely human.

Values: Soulfulness, timelessness and simplicity.

Type

Ligatures and handcrafted letterforms play a central role. By mimicking the organic flow and variation of handwritten strokes, typography becomes a bridge between analog craft and digital clarity.

Designing letterforms on paper allowed us to retain microscopic imperfections—the slight tremble of a line, the uneven taper—that give the experience a warm, human texture. These qualities create a writing system that feels authentically “written,” not generated.

The Line

The line embraces the craft and DNA of writing, with all its perfections and imperfections.

For over a hundred years, the figure 8 has been used by trained calligraphers to test the nibs at the start of every pen's journey. We've brought this iconic gesture to the experience as a way to introduce users to the feel of the device.

Buttons & Links

UI elements used repeatedly—such as pagination indicators or highlight states—carry subtle stroke variations to emulate ink pressure. Starting points, end points, and stroke widths adjust organically, reinforcing the tactile feel of handwriting throughout the interface.

This creates a system where even functional UI elements carry the warmth and rhythm of pen-on-paper writing.

Iconography

Our icons reflect the essence of the line — capturing subtle imperfections from pen pressure and angle. While they nod to the tactile nature of handwriting, they remain clean and modern, balancing handcrafted warmth with UI clarity.

Colour

Our colour system is optimized for eInk, using a refined grayscale palette to ensure clarity, contrast, and a quiet, premium feel.

Layouts & Grids

The layout takes inspiration from Montblanc's symbolic 4810m height — using a 4/8/10 column grid to subtly embed the brand into the interface structure.

Illustrations

These are visual direction and references to guide the illustration style. We aim to strike a balance between function and emotion. Handwritten lines reflect the practical side of note-taking, while hand-sketched elements bring emotional depth and crafted sophistication.

Interaction Model

We defined the interaction model through research into existing eInk devices and real-world handwriting behaviours.

The focus: an intuitive, unobtrusive flow that keeps writing at the centre. Interactions feel intentional yet effortless—always supporting the user's creative or practical task.

Onboarding Flow

The onboarding experience blends Montblanc storytelling with light, playful touchpoints. Users are gradually introduced to key device features, guided through the writing feel, and shown the metaphors rooted in Montblanc craft — such as nib-testing gestures and pressure-responsive strokes.

Through extensive testing of structure, timing, and clarity, the flow became both educational and delightfully welcoming — making eInk technology feel instantly familiar.

Home

The home screen expresses Montblanc's refined craftsmanship through its restrained visual language. Handcrafted icons and delicate linework introduce tactility without overwhelming the space.

A floating toolbar anchors key actions while maintaining a clutter-free layout.

Writing

The writing environment is designed for full immersion. Tools remain hidden until needed, allowing the page to become the purest expression of digital handwriting.

A subtle brand reference lives in the toolbar icon, where the interaction echoes the twist mechanism of certain Montblanc pens, tying back to the physical rituals of writing:
Hollow dot = tools hidden
Filled dot = tools active
