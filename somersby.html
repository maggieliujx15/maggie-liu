Client Context

Somersby is a Denmark Cider brand founded in 2008. They quickly established themselves as a culture of optimism and happiness of bringing people together.

Problem

With their new brand positioning as optimistic and joyful, Somersby needed their online presence to reflect that.

Project Goals

1 — Introduce and distinguish Somersby's expanding product categories

2 — Provide a delightful experience for users when they are discovering Somersby products

3 — Optimized product detail page that communicates information in an engaging way
