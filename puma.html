PUMA: RUN MY WAY 管我怎么跑

As part of Puma's Hybrid Astro campaign, we created an interactive pop-up pavilion in one of Shanghai's busiest districts, inviting people to experience the shoes through exploration rather than competition. Visitors customized their own Puma avatar, then stepped onto a treadmill where their movements powered a journey through three imaginative virtual worlds—celebrating the idea of running your own way.

Puma Personalization

Before running, users create a personalized Puma avatar to represent themselves in the virtual world. To highlight technology at the forefront, I explored motion gestures for interacting with the personalization screens. Based on research, I selected the most intuitive movements to ensure usability and smooth onboarding. Component layout—size, placement, and distance from the user—was carefully considered so all selections remain easily accessible from a single standing position.
