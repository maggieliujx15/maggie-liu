LUFTHANSA: #LIFECHANGINGPLACES

As the largest German airline, Lufthansa believes that travel has the power to transform. The #LifeChangingPlaces campaign embraces that spirit by helping users discover meaningful destinations inspired in their current surroundings.

We built a mobile web app powered by Google Cloud Vision to analyze a user's photo and match key visual cues to destinations across Lufthansa's network—creating a seamless, almost “magical” reveal of their next potential journey.

  • User Flow
  • Prototyping
  • Custom Content Management System

User and Conversation Flow

We created user and conversation flows that move seamlessly from capture to reveal, supported by a friendly, concise tone aligned with Lufthansa's voice. Each step is designed to guide without overwhelming, making the journey feel natural and inspiring.

Sketches

Campaign introduction: The landing screen gently eases users into the experience using short, digestible phrases. They can choose to watch an inspiring travel film or skip ahead and begin immediately.

Camera access: To maintain the conversational flow, camera permissions are requested within a seamless, integrated transition.

Destination approach 1: Immerses users in their suggested destination through local stories, month-by-month weather, and a clear path to booking.

Destination approach 2: Offers a quick snapshot of the destination with a short introductory blurb, revealing more detail as users interact.

Destination approach 3: Highlights top experiences and must-dos, giving users an immediate sense of what the destination has to offer.

Reflection

What began as a content-heavy experience evolved into a light, curiosity-driven journey—much like my own experience on this project. It was my first time working with Google Cloud Vision, and through learning its capabilities, I was able to craft the “magical” moments in the user experience while also contributing to the development of a custom CMS to manage dynamic content.
