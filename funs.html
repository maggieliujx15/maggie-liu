Workshop Artbook

December 2019

Using available materials from The Other James Baldwin workshops to design an Artbook. Workshop participants create their own redacted poetry that re-contextualizes problematic historical texts. Large typography was used throughout the book to reflect the tone and challenges discussed in the workshops.
